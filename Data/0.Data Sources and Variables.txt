EducationGDPspending (percentage of country's GDP spent on education).
	Source:
Source provided by World Bank Group (2024). 
"Government expenditure on education, total (% of GDP)" [dataset]. Local currency into US$.
UNESCO Institute for Statistics (UIS). UIS.Stat Bulk Data Download Service [original].
	Link:
https://databank.worldbank.org/metadataglossary/world-development-indicators/series/SE.XPD.TOTL.GD.ZS


AvYearsSchooling (the average years of schooling)
	Source:
UNDP, Human Development Report (2024) – with minor processing by Our World in Data [dataset].
UNDP relies on: Barro and Lee (2018), ICF Macro Demographic and Health Surveys (various years), OECD (2023),
UNESCO Institute for Statistics (2023) and UNICEF Multiple Indicator Cluster Surveys (various years) [original].
	Link:
https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/average-years-of-schooling?tab=table&time=1999..latest


Continent (on which continent each country is located)
	Source:
Kaggle. Countries by Continent (2021).
	Link:
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/hserdaraltan/countries-by-continent/data


IncomeGroup (seperating countries into income groups by GNI per capita)
	Source:
The World Bank (2024), historical classification by income group. Local currency into US$.
	Link:
https://datahelpdesk.worldbank.org/knowledgebase/articles/906519-world-bank-country-and-lending-groups


UnemploymentRate (percentage of people in a country that are unemployed)
	Source:
Multiple sources compiled by World Bank (2024) – processed by Our World in Data.
“Unemployment, total (% of total labor force) (modeled ILO estimate)” [dataset].
International Labour Organization, “World Development Indicators” [original data].
Human Mortality Database, “Human Mortality Database”; United Nations, “World Population Prospects 2022”;
United Nations, “World Population Prospects”; Zijdeman et al., “Life Expectancy at birth 2”;
James C. Riley, “Estimates of Regional and Global Life Expectancy, 1800-2001” [original].
	Link:
https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/unemployment-rate


LifeExp (Life expectancy at birth by country)
	Source:
OECD (2023) – with minor processing by Our World in Data.
“Life expectancy at birth – OECD – period tables” [dataset].
OECD, “Life expectancy at birth” [original].
	Link:
https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/life-expectancy-at-birth-oecd


UrbanizationRate (share of people living urban areas)
	Source:
Hannah Ritchie, Veronika Samborska and Max Roser (2024) - “Urbanization”.
Published online at OurWorldinData.org. Retrieved from: 'https://ourworldindata.org/urbanization' [Online Resource].
	Link:
https://ourworldindata.org/urbanization


educationLevel (seperating average years of schooling in three categories)
No source - dividing available years of Schooling data into: <=5 is low, <=10 is middle, else is high
